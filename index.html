BrewCrate Press Kit

Everything you need to know to write about BrewCrate.

BrewCrate brings you Homebrew as a Service. Everything you need to brew your own beer month after month.

Interest in craft beer and homebrew is at an all-time high. New breweries are opening at a rate of over 1 per day in the US, but very few would-be homebrewers make it past the first batch. The culprits: bad kit instructions, low-quality ingredients, and an assumption that new brewers know where to find the information they lack. The customer is there, but the product is not. Homebrewing provides a cheap and fun way to socialize, so naturally we wanted a simple way to enjoy this hobby without the frustration of having to roll our own.

So we built BrewCrate. By leveraging our experience in the tech startup scene we are focusing on a great user experience and a great final product (beer). We provide innovative, tested recipes with thorough instructions delivered to your door once a month. For $20 you get all the ingredients necessary to brew a 12-pack of beer, and we also offer a one-time equpiment kit for first-timers, complete with all the supplies required to get started.

Unlike existing offerings, we make sure you're always brewing by providing a variety of high quality, tested recipes and delivering them monthly so you never run out. And rather than focusing on brewers we are focusing on the entire DIY crowd, from hardware hackers to knitters to foodies to LARPers to bored stay-at-home parents. However, we've been told many times that our kits are solid enough to appeal to expert brewers looking for new recipes. If you like making things, you'll love BrewCrate.

While our initial offering is a single kit, we are quickly adding new kit tracks to teach technique and larger kits for those who feel confident in their skills. Additionally, we are building a system for subscribers to decide the style, recipe, and name of each kit in the future, similar to the way Threadless allows users to vote on T-shirt designs. However, our real killer feature is a new take on the e-commerce experience: we are building an end-to-end kit builder that selects ingredients based on your intended brew and makes ordering as simple as searching on Google. Buying a kit this way is unprecedented and empowers the brewer to use their creativity to experiment with new ideas without needing a formal education in the science of brewing.
